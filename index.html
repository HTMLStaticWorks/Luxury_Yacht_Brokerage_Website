Frequently Asked Questions

What are the primary costs of yacht ownership?

Ownership costs typically include crew salaries, dockage, insurance, maintenance, and fuel, usually totaling 10% of the purchase price annually.

How long does the yacht selling process take?

Depending on market conditions and price, yacht sales can take anywhere from 6 to 18 months. Our marketing strategy aims to reduce this time.

Can I charter my yacht to offset costs?

Yes, many owners place their yachts in our charter management program to generate revenue and offset operational expenses.

What is the difference between a motor and sailing yacht?

Motor yachts focus on speed, volume, and luxury amenities, while sailing yachts offer a more traditional, engaged nautical experience.

Does Ocean Elite handle international deliveries?

Yes, we coordinate global logistics, including transatlantic shipping and professional delivery crews.
