Our Heritage

A Journey of Thirty Years

Founded in 1995 in the heart of Monaco, Ocean Elite began as a boutique brokerage with a simple vision: to redefine the luxury maritime experience. Over three decades, we have expanded our reach across five continents, representing the world's most iconic superyachts and most discerning owners.

Our history is marked by record-breaking sales and pioneering charter programs that have set new benchmarks for the industry. Today, we stand as a global leader, yet our core values of discretion and excellence remain unchanged.
